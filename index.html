The Secret Sonant SauceTM

Studio Sonant is an independent game developer collective fueled by snark and potato chips. We mostly procrastinate by complaining that no one really makes the games that we want, then forget to actually be productive. Usually by that point we have also lost any and all motivation, so we spiral into a round of regret that lasts a couple of months. 

But then when we play Thief, Quake, Snatcher or some other thing for the umpteenth time again, we're suddenly back to loving games and continuing on with development. Don't you worry though, we're sure to release something... eventually...
